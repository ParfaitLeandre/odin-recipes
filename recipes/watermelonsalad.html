Watermelon Salad

Description

This watermelon salad is a surprising yet delicious combination of ingredients! Perfect for a summer appetizer or light meal. This refreshing watermelon salad recipe is flavorful, fun, and fruity.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sliced red onion, cut lengthwise
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 15 cups cubed watermelon
  • 3 cups cubed English cucumber
  • 1 (8 ounce) package feta cheese, crumbled
  • Â½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • cracked black pepper, to taste
  • sea salt, to taste

Steps/Directions

  1. Place red onion slices in a small bowl; pour over lime juice. Allow onions to marinate while assembling the salad.
  2. Gently combine watermelon, cucumber, feta cheese, and cilantro in a large bowl; season with black pepper. Toss watermelon salad with marinated onions and season with sea salt just before serving.
